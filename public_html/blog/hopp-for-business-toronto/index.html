What is Hopp for Business?

Hopp for Business is a corporate ride-hailing solution that streamlines how companies manage employee transportation. Instead of employees using personal ride apps and submitting receipts afterward, the business profile handles everything centrally:

  • Centralized billing for all company rides
  • Spending rules and limits that finance and procurement teams can set
  • Consolidated invoicing in one place
  • Automated receipt generation for every trip
  • Expense-management integrations with popular platforms

The platform also includes a Ride Booker feature that lets companies book and schedule travel for employees, partners, or guests - even if the passenger doesn’t use the Hopp app themselves.

How it works for businesses

  1. Setup: the company creates a business profile and sets spending rules.
  2. Team onboarding: employees, partners, or guests are added to the business account (one by one or via bulk upload).
  3. Booking: rides are booked through the app or via Ride Booker for non-app users.
  4. Tracking: every trip appears on centralized billing with automated receipts.
  5. Reconciliation: consolidated invoicing makes expense reconciliation faster at month-end.

Who should use it

Hopp for Business is a good fit for:

  • SMBs with GTA offices that need reliable employee transportation.
  • Companies with multiple GTA locations that run inter-office travel.
  • Remote and hybrid teams that meet in person across the Greater Toronto Area.
  • Businesses with client meetings that need dependable, on-brand transportation.
  • Finance teams wanting tighter control and visibility over travel spending.

It’s especially useful for companies that currently wrestle with scattered ride receipts, manual expense reporting, or no spending visibility at all.
