What is the Kit MCP?

MCP stands for Model Context Protocol - an open standard that lets AI assistants connect to external tools and data sources in real time. Kit’s implementation of it is a secure connection between your AI tool of choice (Claude, ChatGPT, Cursor, Microsoft Copilot, and others) and your actual Kit email account.

Think of it as the plug that finally lets your AI and your email business talk to each other. Instead of switching back into Kit every time you want to pull data or take action, you stay in the AI conversation: ask a question, get a real answer drawn from your actual account, and act on it - all in one place.

What your AI can actually do with Kit MCP

Most platforms that offer AI integrations provide read-only access - the AI can look at your data but cannot do anything about it. Kit’s MCP offers both read and write access. That means your AI tool can:

  • Pull subscriber data, tags, segments, and performance metrics from your live account.
  • Analyze broadcast open rates and click data without you digging through dashboards.
  • Draft and schedule broadcasts from idea to queued email, without opening Kit.
  • Tag subscribers and enroll them in sequences the moment an insight surfaces.

The read-plus-write combination is what separates insight from action. Finding out which subscribers haven’t opened anything in 90 days is useful. Being able to immediately enroll them in a re-engagement sequence from the same conversation is where it becomes a time saver.
