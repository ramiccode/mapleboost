Cookies & analytics

We use cookies to make the site work and to understand how it’s used. This includes Google Analytics, a third-party service that sets cookies to measure traffic and page performance, and affiliate-tracking cookies that record when you click through to a partner so we can be credited for any resulting sign-up. These do not identify you by name to us.

You can block or delete cookies in your browser settings at any time; the site will still work, though some analytics will not be collected. You can also change your analytics choice anytime via “Cookie settings” in the footer. We do not sell your personal information.
