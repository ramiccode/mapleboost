The Maple Stack

One tool we’d actually use - per job

Most of our site compares options side by side. This page does the opposite. For each part of running a business in Canada, we name a single pick - the one we’d reach for - and skip the comparison table entirely. If we wouldn’t recommend it to a friend who just started out, it isn’t here.

Want the full trade-offs and runners-up for a category? Those live on our reviews pages. The Maple Stack is for when you just want a decision.

How we choose a pick

One per category, on purpose. We weigh how fast it is to get started, whether it fits the way Canadian businesses actually operate (sales tax, local support, data residency), and whether it keeps making sense as you grow. We disclose every commercial relationship - the partnership funds the work but doesn’t buy a spot. When a better option overtakes the incumbent, the pick changes.
