Why we built these

The CRA and Revenu Québec calculators are accurate but slow and unfriendly. The free third-party calculators are usually adware. We wanted clean, fast, no-signup tools that match the current rates - and that we’d use ourselves.

How accurate are they?

The math is correct. The rates are reviewed every quarter against the published CRA and provincial schedules. If you spot a discrepancy - especially after a budget announcement - please tell us.

Not a substitute for professional advice

These tools are for planning and education. For anything you’re actually filing, use the CRA’s official tools or talk to a CPA. We have a full disclaimer on our editorial policy page.
